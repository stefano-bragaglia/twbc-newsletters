\headline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily Potting Shed Gossip:}}\\
          {\textbf{\large\sffamily Bend it like... Fishwick?!?}}}
\label{2025-02-gossip}
\noindent
This month, we're tipping our hats (and pruning shears) to \textbf{David Fishwick}, who continues to dominate the \textit{Tree of the Month} competition and surprise us with his impeccable bonsai skills.
At our recent meetup, David left us all on the edge of our seats as he attempted a \textbf{daring bend on a hawthorn with a trunk as thick as a wrist}.
During Steve's grafting workshop, David identified the base of the trunk where he wanted the bend, cut out a precise wedge, and inserted screws above and below the gap.
Using a wire between the screws, he tensioned the trunk into position, creating a dramatic curve.
To protect the area, he carefully taped it and reinforced it with heavy wire --- a masterclass in precision and nerve!
We're all eagerly waiting to see how this bold move progresses and what further bends he has planned for the top of the tree.

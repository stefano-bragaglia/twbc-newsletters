\headline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily Club Talent on Display:}}\\
          {\textbf{\large\sffamily Twickenham Members at the K2 Bonsai Expo}}}
\label{2025-05-show}
\noindent
April brought with it one of the most anticipated events in the UK bonsai calendar---the \textbf{K2 Bonsai Expo}, held on 13 April 2025 at the K2 Leisure Centre in Crawley, West Sussex.
Known for its high standards and attracting some of the finest displays from across the country, the Expo represents an opportunity for bonsai enthusiasts to showcase their trees on a national platform.

We are proud to announce that this year, \textbf{three members of the Twickenham Bonsai Club} had a total of \textbf{four trees accepted for display}.
This is no small feat, and it reflects both the quality of individual craftsmanship and the supportive, knowledge\--sharing environment that defines our club.
For a local club like ours to have such visibility at a national exhibition is not only a cause for celebration, but also a strong testament to the depth of talent growing right here in our community.

Let us take a closer look at each of the trees exhibited---each with its own story, challenges, and character---as told by the members who cultivated them.
Seeing our members take their trees to such heights is both inspiring and motivating.
Who knows how many more of us will make it to the next big show?
Until then, let's keep nurturing our trees---and each other.

\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily May Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Spring Into Shape: New Growth, New Possibilities}}}{The Newsletter Team}
\vspace*{-0.5em}
\noindent
May's meeting brought a wonderful turnout, with \textbf{22 members} gathering to share another evening of bonsai camaraderie. 
The room buzzed with conversation and the soft rustling of foliage, as friends old and new reconnected around their trees.

\medskip
After the excitement of hosting Corin from Greenwood Bonsai last month, we found ourselves back to the essentials.
This month, we were treated to a talk and not one but two excellent workshops: \textbf{Mark Angold} told us more about his \textit{bonsai journey}, \textbf{Steve Palmer} guided us through the art and techniques of \textit{air\--layering}, sharing practical advice and personal tips, while \textbf{Adam} demonstrated the intricate craft of \textit{wood root stand carving}, showing how to create beautiful, naturalistic displays for our trees.
All sessions offered something valuable for members at every stage of their bonsai journey.

\medskip
As always, the \textbf{Tree of the Month} and \textbf{Novice Tree of the Month} competitions brought out some wonderful entries.
This month's theme, \textit{``Any Flowering Tree''}, filled the room with colour and scent, showcasing the beauty of flowering species.
Alongside the contest, we also enjoyed an informal \textbf{Mame bonsai display}, where members brought in their tiniest creations, for fun and admiration.

\medskip
A special congratulations goes to three of our members who proudly exhibited four trees at the prestigious \textbf{K2 Bonsai Expo} --- a major highlight in the UK bonsai calendar.
More details on these displays can be found inside the newsletter.

\medskip
Thank you to everyone who came along and helped make it another memorable evening.
We look forward to seeing even more of you next month!

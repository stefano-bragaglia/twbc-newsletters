    \noindent
    Our last club night was both joyful and reflective as we paused to honor the late \textbf{Andy Pearson} of \emph{Stone Monkey Ceramics}, who passed away unexpectedly last week.
    Renowned for his exquisite handmade pots, particularly in Shohin, Kifu, and Chuhin sizes, Andrew's artistry and contributions to the community will be profoundly missed.
\end{window}

In January 2014, Andrew made history as the first Western potter to participate in Japan's prestigious \textbf{10th Annual Modern Shohin Container Artist Exhibition} at Gafu\--Ten, where his unglazed trio of pots earned the gold prize---a testament to his exceptional talent and dedication.

Andrew's passion extended beyond his craft, as he shared his knowledge generously through \href{https://www.instagram.com/stonemonkey1968/}{Instagram}, \href{https://www.youtube.com/@stonemonkey1968}{YouTube}, and personal connections.
His legacy will continue to inspire bonsai enthusiasts worldwide.

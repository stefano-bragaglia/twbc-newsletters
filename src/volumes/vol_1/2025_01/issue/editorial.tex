\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily January Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Roadmap to the Winter Show}}}{The Newsletter Team}
\noindent
The January meeting of the Twickenham Bonsai Club was a lively and engaging evening, with an attendance of 22 members.
As everyone arrived, they eagerly set up their trees for the \textbf{Tree of the Month} contest.
The competition was fierce, with many beautiful specimens on display, showcasing the talent and dedication of our members.

The highlight of the evening was an insightful talk by \textbf{Alex Rudd}, who shared his expert knowledge on the \emph{key criteria for selecting the perfect pot to complement a bonsai}.
Alex’s thorough understanding and clear explanations impressed everyone, and his tips proved to be extremely helpful for both beginners and seasoned enthusiasts alike.

During the break, members enjoyed a chance to chat informally, with plenty of reminders for the upcoming \textbf{TWBC Winter Show}.
Set for \emph{Sunday, 2 February 2025}, the event will be held at \emph{St Richard Reynolds Catholic College, Clifden Road, Twickenham, TW1 4LT}.
More information is available inside!

As the evening drew to a close, members cast their votes for their favourite trees in the contest.
The winners will be announced and celebrated during the Winter Show, making for a memorable conclusion to an exciting night.
A big thank you to everyone who participated and helped make this meeting such a success.
We look forward to more fantastic bonsai moments in the coming months!

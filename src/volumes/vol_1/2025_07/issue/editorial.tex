\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily July Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Quiet Critique, Hot Days, and Summer Glory}}}{The Newsletter Team}  
\noindent  
After the excitement of last month's star\--studded workshop, July's meetup brought a shift in pace.
A solid turnout gathered for a quieter, more focused evening of in\--depth critique.
Members brought in trees for thoughtful discussion and technical feedback, sparking some excellent conversations on design, balance, and seasonal care.
(See inside for Tina Todd's personal take on the night and the value of constructive critique.)

\medskip  
But while the club night was low\--key, the wider bonsai scene is buzzing.
With summer now in full swing---and the heat to prove it---we've seen a flurry of shows, events, and well\--earned accolades.
Our own \textbf{Bonsai Summer Show} returned with all the hallmarks of the Winter edition, enhanced by two dedicated tables of exquisite \textbf{Mame bonsai} and a full\--day demonstration station that kept the atmosphere lively and informative.
You'll find more coverage inside.

\medskip  
Meanwhile, the \textbf{UKBA Collective Display} at the \textbf{Hampton Court Palace Garden Festival} earned the top\--tier \textbf{Tudor Rose Award}, doubling down on the earlier success at the Chelsea Flower Show.
Several of our members contributed to the display---a point of real pride for the club, and a wonderful showcase of national\--level talent and teamwork.

\medskip  
More events are rolling in across the country, with club members showing trees, supporting exhibits, and sharing the spirit of bonsai wherever they go.
Just remember to water deeply (your trees and yourselves!) as temperatures soar.

\medskip  
As always, thank you to everyone who joined us this month---your presence, questions, and quiet enthusiasm keep our community connected.
Whether you came for feedback, inspiration, or just good company, you help the club thrive.

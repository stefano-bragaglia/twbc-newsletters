\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily September Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Welcoming New Faces and Evergreen Excellence}}}{The Newsletter Team}
\vspace*{-0.5em}
\noindent
Though the holiday season has passed, a few familiar faces were notably absent, yet our September club night boasted an exceptionally high turnout. Twenty-seven members gathered at the Whitton Community Centre, including two fresh faces we’re thrilled to welcome: \textbf{Dante} and \textbf{Mickey}! It’s always exciting to see new enthusiasts join our community and share in the joy of bonsai.

The undisputed highlight of the evening was a special visit from \textbf{Peter Warren}, who generously lent his expertise in critiquing a selection of members’ trees. For those new to the name, Peter Warren is an internationally renowned bonsai artist and author based here in the UK, celebrated for his nursery \href{https://saruyama.co.uk}{Saruyama} and his tireless efforts to make bonsai accessible through education. You’ll find a deeper dive into Peter’s visit and insights inside this issue.

Also featured in this newsletter is a brief report from the \textbf{East Anglia Bonsai Show}, where our chairman \textbf{Tony Ulatowski} proudly presented a striking Hinoki display on behalf of our club: a proud moment that showed our passion and skill.

As always, the \textbf{Tree of the Month} competition took centre stage, with evergreens shining brightly under the spotlight. We were delighted to receive many stunning entries, though it seems some of our newer members opted for a well-earned sabbatical this month.

\medskip
Looking forward, don’t miss our next club night on \textbf{Thursday 9th October} at the \textbf{Whitton Community Centre}. The \textbf{Tree of the Month} theme will be \textit{``Autumn colours, any tree''} — a perfect opportunity to showcase the season’s vibrant hues. Whether seasoned or just bonsai-curious, come join us for another evening of learning, sharing, and growing together.

\headline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily TWBC Club Reminders:}}\\
          {\textbf{\large\sffamily Visit to RHS Wisley and Sutton Bonsai Show}}}
\label{calendar}
\noindent
Just a final reminder that our club visit to \textbf{RHS Wisley} is happening on \textbf{Saturday, 20th September}. This is also a great opportunity to enjoy the Sutton Club's bonsai show, which will be featured on\--site during our visit.

\medskip
We currently have \textbf{20 attendees}, including \textbf{3 UKBA members from other clubs}. If final numbers haven't been confirmed with RHS yet, you might be still in time to join or bring a guest. Should that be the case, please get in touch with our Club Secretary \textbf{Nick Lloyd} as soon as possible.

\medskip
We'll be meeting outside the main entrance in the car park between 10:00 and 10:15am. Please arrive on time, as we must enter the gardens as a group. Don't be late!

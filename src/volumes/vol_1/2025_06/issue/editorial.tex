\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily June Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Forests, Fame, and Fresh Techniques}}}{The Newsletter Team}  
\noindent  
This month's meetup was a true highlight of the club's calendar, as we welcomed the internationally renowned \textbf{Caroline Scott} (\href{https://www.instagram.com/cazbonsai/}{\textit{@cazbonsai}}) for a day of hands-on bonsai magic. \textbf{Caz} led an immersive workshop, helping members refine their trees with her expert eye, followed by an evening talk delving into evergreens and the art of \textit{mekiri}---a masterclass in precision and seasonal technique.
Her energy and generosity left everyone inspired, from newcomers to seasoned stylists.

\medskip  
The \textbf{Tree of the Month} competitions transformed the room into a miniature woodland, with the theme \textit{``Groupings, Forests''} inspiring creative interpretations of group plantings.
From delicate sapling arrangements to weathered multi-trunk compositions, each entry told a story of patience and vision.
The \textbf{Novice category} surprised us all with its sophistication---proof that great bonsai starts with bold beginnings. % Special mention goes to \textbf{Linda Hartley}'s larch group, whose naturalistic flow earned particular admiration during the evening's lively discussions.

\medskip  
Beyond our club walls, two of our members flew the flag high at the prestigious \textbf{FOBBS New Talent Competition}. \textbf{David Fishwick} and \textbf{Mark Pittman} competed to impress judges by first\--styling junipers that balanced raw material with refined technique.
Their participation reflects the growing reputation of our club as a nurturing ground for emerging artists---we couldn't be prouder!

\medskip  
A warm welcome once again to our new member, Graham, and a big thank you to all 26 members who joined in, asked questions, and shared their passion.
Whether you left with a freshly styled tree, new knowledge, or simply a smile, your enthusiasm makes this club thrive.

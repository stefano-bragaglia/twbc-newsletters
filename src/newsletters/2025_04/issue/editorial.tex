\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily April Bonsai Club Meetup:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily A Day with Corin Tomlinson: Bonsai Masterclass and Demo}}}{The Newsletter Team}
\vspace*{-0.5em}
\noindent
April's meeting was a standout event, with \textbf{24 members} filling the room---proof of the growing enthusiasm in our club.
The energy was palpable as attendees shared stories and techniques, but the undeniable highlight was the rare opportunity to learn from \textbf{Corin Tomlinson} of Greenwood Bonsai, a celebrated figure in the UK bonsai scene.

\medskip
Corin's \textbf{afternoon workshop} was a masterclass in artistry and patience.
He guided members in styling their trees while transforming a \textbf{striking juniper} into a future masterpiece.
The club raffled this tree, and though \textit{Chris Hallewell} couldn't attend to claim his prize, we'll ensure it finds its way to him at the \textbf{K2 Bonsai Expo}.
Corin's \textbf{evening demo} was equally captivating, as he styled two more trees with precision, sharing trade secrets that left everyone scribbling notes.

\medskip
The \textbf{Tree of the Month} competition brought conifers into the spotlight with the theme \textbf{``Juniper, Cypress, or Hemlock.''} Nine stellar entries---six advanced and three novice---showcased the diversity of these rugged species, sparking lively discussions about their unique challenges and charms.

\medskip
Looking ahead, the \textbf{K2 Bonsai Expo} in Crawley (13th April 2025, K2 Leisure Centre, RH11 9BQ) promises to be a marquee event for our club, with Tony's and Nick's trees on display.
Meanwhile, those who attended the \textbf{Mini Bonsai at Chobham Village} returned brimming with inspiration from the delicate Shohin and Mame displays---more on that inside!

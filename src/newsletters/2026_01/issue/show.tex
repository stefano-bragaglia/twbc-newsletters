\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily Bonsai Events:}}\\
        {\textbf{\large\sffamily Twickenham Bonsai Club Winter Show}}}
       {Stefano Bragaglia}
\phantomsection
\label{2026-01-show}
\noindent
\textit{Sunday, 1st February 2026} \\
\textit{St Richard Reynolds Catholic College, Clifden Road, Twickenham. TW1 4LT.}

\medskip
On Sunday 1st February, Twickenham Bonsai Club returned to St Richard Reynolds Catholic College for their annual Winter Show.
Despite the cold weather, the event was a resounding success, attracting a steady stream of visitors and enthusiasts who braved the winter chill to view the trees in their winter silhouettes.

\medskip
The atmosphere was buzzing throughout the day, particularly in the workshop and trade area where members were busy demonstrating various techniques, including bold structural bending, wiring, and the creation of large forest compositions on custom slabs.
A special highlight of the afternoon was the presentation of an \textbf{RHS certificate} to \textbf{Tina Todd}, in recognition of her tireless contributions to the club and the wider bonsai hobby.
%\columnbreak

\medskip
The show featured a high standard of member\--owned trees, alongside a specialist \textbf{larch progression display}, \textbf{suiseki stones}, and an exquisite \textbf{mame section}.
Also on display was a new \textbf{club yearbook}, documenting a full year of the club's newsletters and activities.
The event was well\--supported by traders including \textbf{Nick Payne} (\href{https://www.instagram.com/springwood_ceramics/}{Springwood Ceramics}), \textbf{Neil Hutchings} (\href{htthttps://www.instagram.com/devon_bonsai_nursery/}{Devon Bonsai Nursery}), \textbf{Vinay Rathore} (\href{https://www.instagram.com/tokoen.bonsai.uk}{Toko-en Bonsai}), and \textbf{Stuart Beresford}.

\medskip
The results of the public vote for favourite 3 trees in show was:

\begin{itemize}
    \item 1st Place was \textbf{Terry Wilson}'s \textit{Chamaecyparis pisifera `Boulevard'}

    \item 2nd Place was \textbf{Mark Edwards}'s \textit{Acer palmatum `Arakawa'}

    \item 3rd Place was \textbf{Liz Panton}'s \textit{Acer palmatum `Shishigashira'}.
\end{itemize}

\medskip
Many thanks to all the members who helped with the setup and to the \textbf{UKBA}, \textbf{FoBBS}, and the \textbf{RHS} for endorsing the event.

\medskip
\hfill\textit{Stefano Bragaglia}

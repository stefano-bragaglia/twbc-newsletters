\byline{{\textbf{\Large\sffamily Bonsai Events:}}\\
{\textbf{\large\sffamily Twickenham Bonsai Club Summer Show}}}{Nick Lloyd}
\label{2025-07-show}
\noindent
\textit{Sunday, 29th June 2025} \\
\textit{St Richard Reynolds Catholic College, Clifden Road, Twickenham. TW1 4LT.}

\medskip
This year Twickenham Bonsai Club hosted their 1st ever Summer show, having previously put together similar winter events over the last few years.
This was a new experience for some members showing their trees in leaf for the first time and having to contend with the hot weather we have been experiencing over the last few weeks.

\medskip
The day itself was another warm one, and as with previous shows, there was a lively atmosphere with members keen to share their knowledge of bonsai to anyone who wanted to listen.
Club members were busy working on trees and answering questions throughout the day, with \textbf{Andy} from the \href{https://thebonsaished.com}{Bonsai Shed} doing a demonstration in the afternoon.
\columnbreak

\medskip
The show included around 50 trees on display, a progression table walking through stages in development of an English elm, and separate \textbf{mame} and \textbf{suiseki displays}.
The show was also supported by traders from the \href{https://thebonsaished.com}{Bonsai Shed}, \href{https://www.facebook.com/p/Collettes-Bonsai-100063721504968/}{Collette’s Bonsai} and \href{https://www.springwoodceramics.co.uk}{Springwood ceramics}.

\medskip
The results of the public vote for favourite 3 trees in show was:

\begin{itemize}
    \item 1st Place was \textbf{Mark Edward}'s \textit{Beni Chidori Maple}

    \item 2nd Place was \textbf{Mark Angold}'s \textit{Chinese Elm}

    \item 3rd Place was \textbf{Tony Ulatowski}'s \textit{Trident Maple}.
\end{itemize}

\medskip
Many thanks to all who supported the show.

\medskip
\hfill\textit{Nick Lloyd}
